About

It is lovely to e-meet you. I'm a graduate student studying for a Masters in Global Security and Borders for a year at Queen's University Belfast in Northern Ireland. I am originally from Connecticut in the United States. Previously, I have spent time living in Washington D.C. and London. I am a curious and outgoing individual who is passioante about travel, learning, and meeting new people. My hope is that through this blog, I can create a platform to share my experiences and start a conversation through sharing what's inspired, awed, and challenged me.
